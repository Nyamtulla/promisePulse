EAST WARD ROADS - PROGRESS UPDATE
Public Works Department
April 15, 2025

Following the Mayor's March announcement regarding road repairs in East Ward:

Work has begun on Main Street. Pothole filling is 40% complete. Crews are currently working on the section between Oak Lane and Maple Drive. Resurfacing is scheduled to start next week.

The drainage improvement along Main Street is in progress. New culverts have been installed at three critical points. We expect the drainage work to be finished by end of May.

Residents have reported improved conditions in the completed sections. We remain on track to meet our Q3 deadline for full completion.

For updates, contact the Public Works hotline.
