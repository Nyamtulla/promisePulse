DOWNTOWN COMMUNITY CENTER - CONSTRUCTION UPDATE
City of Lawrence
October 2025

Update on the community center project pledged in City Council Resolution 2025-042:

RESULT: Construction is underway. Groundbreaking took place in August as planned. Foundation work is complete. Steel framing for the main building is 60% erected. The library wing and meeting room block are taking shape. On-site work is on schedule for the December 2026 completion target.

The project remains within budget. We have fulfilled our commitment to break ground in Q3 2025 and are on track to deliver the downtown community center as promised.
