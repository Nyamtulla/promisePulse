RIVERSIDE PARK RENOVATION - COMPLETED
Parks & Recreation Department
June 2025

The park renovation project, pledged in the 2024 municipal campaign, is now complete.

RESULT: All planned improvements have been delivered. The playground has been fully rebuilt with accessible equipment. New walking paths (2.3 km total) are open. The basketball courts have been resurfaced, and the community garden expansion added 24 new plots. Lighting upgrades are installed throughout.

Usage has increased 35% since reopening. The department has received positive feedback from residents. We have fulfilled our campaign promise to modernize Riverside Park by summer 2025.
