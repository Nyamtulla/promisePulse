NORTH CORRIDOR BUS STOPS - CONSTRUCTION BEGINS
Transport Department
May 2025

As promised in the North Sector Development Announcement:

Construction has started on the 8 new covered bus stops along the North Corridor route. Foundations have been laid at 3 locations. The first bus stop (North Gate) is expected to be operational by end of June.

Each stop will include:
- Weather shelter
- Seating for 12 passengers
- Real-time bus arrival displays
- Accessibility ramps

We remain committed to our September 2025 completion target for all 8 bus stops.
