# Council Drainage Initiative 2025

## Public Works Committee Resolution

The following infrastructure promises were approved in the February 2025 session:

### Drainage Systems
- **Drainage overhaul in Sector 7**: All clogged drains will be cleared and new gratings installed by June 2025
- **Flood prevention**: Construction of retention basins in low-lying areas of East Ward
- **Stormwater management**: We commit to completing the stormwater channel upgrade along River Road

### Sanitation
- **Public toilet facilities**: New sanitation blocks at bus stops 12, 15, and 18
- **Waste collection**: Promise to increase garbage pickup frequency in residential zones from twice to thrice weekly

### Streetlights
- Solar-powered streetlights will be installed on all major pedestrian paths in the downtown area.
