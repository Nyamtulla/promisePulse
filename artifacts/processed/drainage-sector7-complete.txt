SECTOR 7 DRAINAGE - COMPLETION REPORT
Public Works Department
May 2025

The drainage overhaul in Sector 7, as promised in the Council's February resolution, is now complete.

All clogged drains have been cleared. New gratings have been installed at 47 locations. The retention basin construction in the low-lying area of Sector 7 is finished and operational.

Residents reported no flooding during the April showers. The stormwater channel along River Road has been upgraded and is functioning as designed.

We have delivered on our promise to complete the Sector 7 drainage initiative by June 2025.
