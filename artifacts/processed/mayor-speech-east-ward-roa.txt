MAYOR'S ADDRESS TO EAST WARD RESIDENTS
March 2025

Dear citizens of East Ward,

I am pleased to announce our infrastructure commitments for this year.

First, I want to assure you that the roads in East Ward will be repaired by the end of Q3. We have allocated 2 million for pothole repairs and resurfacing of Main Street and Oak Avenue. The drainage along these roads will also be improved to prevent flooding during monsoon season.

Secondly, we will install 50 new streetlights along the bus routes serving East Ward. This will improve safety for commuters and pedestrians.

Third, the water supply system in the northern sector will be upgraded. We promise to complete the pipeline replacement project by December 2025.

Thank you for your continued trust.
