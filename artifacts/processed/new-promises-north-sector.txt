NORTH SECTOR DEVELOPMENT ANNOUNCEMENT
April 2025

The Municipal Corporation announces the following commitments for North Sector:

WATER SUPPLY: We promise to complete the pipeline replacement project in North Sector by December 2025. This will ensure 24-hour water supply to 15,000 households.

BUS STOPS: New covered bus stops with seating will be constructed at 8 locations along the North Corridor route. Completion target: September 2025.

SANITATION: Public works will build 4 new community toilet blocks in North Sector's underserved neighborhoods. We commit to completing this by Q4 2025.

ROADS: The link road connecting North Sector to the highway will be widened and resurfaced. Work begins in May.
