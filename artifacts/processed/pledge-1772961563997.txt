CITY OF LAWRENCE - GREEN INFRASTRUCTURE PLEDGE
Sustainable Development Initiative
April 2025

The City Commission commits to the following:

PLEDGE: We will install 2 miles of protected bike lanes along Massachusetts Street and convert 5 city-owned lots into pocket parks by December 2025. This initiative supports our climate action goals and improves quality of life for residents. Budget: $320,000 from the green infrastructure fund.

Timeline:
- Bike lane design and approval: May 2025
- Construction start: June 2025
- Pocket park groundbreakings: July 2025
- Project completion: December 2025

This pledge was approved by the City Commission. We will provide quarterly progress updates to the public.
