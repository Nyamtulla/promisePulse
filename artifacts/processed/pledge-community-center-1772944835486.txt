LAWRENCE CITY COUNCIL - CAMPAIGN PLEDGE
Resolution 2025-042
February 2025

The City Council commits to the following:

PLEDGE: We will construct a new community center in the downtown district by December 2026. The facility will include a library branch, meeting rooms, youth programs, and senior activities. Total investment: $4.2 million. Funding has been allocated in the current capital improvement budget.

Timeline:
- Design phase: Q2 2025
- Groundbreaking: Q3 2025
- Construction completion: December 2026
- Grand opening: January 2027

This pledge was approved unanimously by the City Commission. We are committed to transparent progress updates throughout the project.
