GREEN INFRASTRUCTURE INITIATIVE - COMPLETION REPORT
City of Lawrence
December 2025

Update on the bike lanes and pocket parks pledged in April 2025:

RESULT: All 2 miles of protected bike lanes along Massachusetts Street are complete and open to cyclists. The five pocket parks have been developed and are now operational, featuring native plantings, seating, and public art installations. Construction finished on schedule in December 2025.

Ridership on the new bike lanes has exceeded initial projections. Residents have welcomed the pocket parks as new gathering spaces. We have delivered on our pledge to complete the green infrastructure initiative by December 2025.
