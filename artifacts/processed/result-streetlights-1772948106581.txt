OAK STREET STREETLIGHT PROJECT - COMPLETION REPORT
Public Works Department
August 2025

Update on the streetlight initiative announced in March 2025:

RESULT: All 120 LED streetlights have been installed along the Oak Street corridor. The three previously unlit blocks are now fully illuminated. Replacement of outdated fixtures is complete. Residents report improved visibility and a reduction in after-dark incidents. We have delivered on our pledge to complete the streetlight project by September 2025.
