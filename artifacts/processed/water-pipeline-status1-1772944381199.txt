NORTH SECTOR WATER PIPELINE - STATUS UPDATE
Municipal Water Board
June 2025

Update on the pipeline replacement project announced in April:

Phase 1 (Northern Zone) is 60% complete. New pipelines have been laid along 8 km of the network. Household connections are being upgraded in batches.

Phase 2 will begin in July. We are on schedule to meet our December 2025 completion promise for the full North Sector pipeline replacement.

Residents in completed areas report improved water pressure and clarity. The 24-hour supply target remains our commitment.
