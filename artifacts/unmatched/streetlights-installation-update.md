# Streetlight Installation Update
## East Ward Safety Initiative

**Date:** April 2025  
**Status:** In Progress

As promised in the Mayor's March address, the streetlight installation project is underway.

### Completed
- 15 of 50 planned streetlights have been installed along the bus route on Oak Avenue
- All installed units are solar-powered and operational
- Pedestrian feedback has been positive

### In Progress
- Installation continuing on Main Street bus stops
- Electrical connections for non-solar units in shaded areas

### Timeline
We expect to complete all 50 streetlights by July 2025, ahead of our original schedule.

*This is an update on the East Ward streetlight promise.*
